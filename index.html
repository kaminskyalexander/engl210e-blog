About me

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo with a passion for video games and computer graphics. My experience spans industry-standard software like Unreal Engine and Houdini, where I've built tools for 3D artists and worked on 2D rendering for user interfaces. I've also developed my own OpenGL renderer, combining my academic background with hands-on experience in the field. I started out in high school with game development, which helped me discover my interest in Computer Science. I'm particularly fascinated by what happens behind the scenes in video games and film. When I'm away from my computer, I enjoy hiking, biking, skiing, and kayaking.
